Privacy Policy for SyncMyPhone

Last updated: December 7, 2024

Privacy by Design: SyncMyPhone is built with your privacy as the top priority. All data remains on your device - we do not maintain servers, user accounts, or cloud storage. This policy explains exactly what data we collect and how it is used for digital wellbeing purposes.

About SyncMyPhone

SyncMyPhone is a digital wellbeing Android application designed to help you manage phone usage by intelligently blocking distracting applications while maintaining full functionality of essential applications such as banking and productivity tools.

Data Collection Overview

We collect minimal data necessary for digital wellbeing functionality. All data is stored locally on your device only and never transmitted to external servers.

App Activity Data

What we collect:

  • Application launch events (when you open an application)
  • Application usage duration tracking
  • Blocked application interaction attempts
  • Last accessed timestamps for productivity insights

Collection method:

Through Android's standard UsageStatsManager API (requires "Usage Access" permission)

Purpose:

To monitor application usage and provide intelligent blocking for digital wellbeing

Application Information Data

What we collect:

  • Package names of installed applications (e.g., "com.instagram.android")
  • Application names and icons for display purposes

Collection method:

Through Android's PackageManager system service

Purpose:

To identify which applications to block and display them in our interface

Data We Do Not Collect

SyncMyPhone does not collect the following types of data:

  • Personal identifiable information (names, email addresses, phone numbers)
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data
  • Contacts, photos, or media files
  • Internet browsing history
  • Application content or messages
  • IP addresses or web traffic data

Data Usage

The collected data is used exclusively for digital wellbeing purposes:

  • Application Blocking: To block distracting social media applications when the blocking feature is activated
  • Usage Analytics: To provide insights into your application usage patterns
  • Mindful Interruptions: To provide breathing exercises when you attempt to access blocked applications
  • Feature Functionality: To maintain temporary allow lists and pause functionality

Local Data Storage

Important: SyncMyPhone operates entirely offline. We do not maintain servers, databases, or cloud storage infrastructure. All data remains on your device and is never transmitted externally.

  • All data is stored in an encrypted local database (Room with SQLCipher)
  • No internet connection is required for core functionality
  • No data transmission occurs to external servers
  • Users maintain complete privacy and control over their data

User Account Policy

SyncMyPhone does not create user accounts, require registration, or store personal information on external servers. The application operates completely independently on your device without any authentication requirements.

Data Sharing Policy

We do not share your data with third parties under any circumstances.

  • No third-party analytics services are used
  • No advertising networks have access to data
  • No data brokers or marketing companies receive information
  • No cloud synchronization services are employed

Data Security

Your data security is ensured through multiple layers of protection:

  • Local Encryption: All data is encrypted using SQLCipher technology
  • Minimal Permissions: Only essential Android permissions are requested
  • No Network Access: The application does not connect to the internet
  • Secure Storage: Data is stored in Android's secure application data directory

Android Permissions

Usage Access Permission

Purpose:

Monitor application launches for blocking functionality

Technical implementation:

Uses UsageStatsManager.queryEvents() to detect when applications are opened

Privacy protection:

This is a standard Android permission specifically designed for digital wellbeing applications and does not provide access to personal data

Data Deletion and Control

You maintain complete control over your data through the following methods:

  • Application Uninstall: Removes all data permanently from your device
  • Clear Application Data: Use Android Settings → Apps → SyncMyPhone → Storage → Clear Data
  • In-App Reset: Use the application's reset function to clear usage history and settings

Since all data is stored locally, deletion is immediate and permanent with no external copies retained.

Children's Privacy

SyncMyPhone is designed for adults (18 years and older) seeking digital wellbeing solutions. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 years of age. If you believe a child has used our application, please contact us and we will assist in ensuring proper data handling.

Google Play Data Safety Compliance

This privacy policy fully aligns with our Google Play Data Safety declaration:

  • Application activity data is collected for functionality purposes only
  • Package names are collected for application identification purposes only
  • No data is shared with third parties
  • All data is encrypted and stored locally
  • No user accounts are created or maintained

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our application or legal requirements. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Since the application operates locally, policy changes will not affect existing data stored on your device.

Contact Information

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle data:

  • Email: support@syncmyphone.app
  • Website: SyncMyPhone

Summary: SyncMyPhone collects minimal application usage data locally on your device to provide digital wellbeing features. No data leaves your device, no accounts are created, and you maintain complete control over your information.
